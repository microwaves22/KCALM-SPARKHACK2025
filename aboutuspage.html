About Us

We are KCALM, a dynamic group of five student developers from the University of Illinois Chicago: Cielo Gonzalez, Kendi Langley, Lemuel (LJ) Reyes, and Michelle Ye. Coming from diverse coding backgrounds, we’ve united our skills with a shared passion for empowering small businesses. Our mission is to help local entrepreneurs thrive in the competitive corporate world. Together, we’ve created a website that connects people with local business owners, fostering stronger communities and supporting independent businesses.
